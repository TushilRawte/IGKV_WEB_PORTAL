Student Login Portal

The Student Portal of IGKV (Indira Gandhi Krishi Vishwavidyalaya) serves as a comprehensive platform for students, providing seamless access to academic resources, important announcements, and administrative services. It is designed to support the academic journey by offering features like course registration, examination schedules, and result viewing. Additionally, the portal connects students with essential resources such as library access, scholarship information, and e-learning materials. User-friendly and accessible, the IGKV Student Portal ensures that students have the tools they need to succeed, fostering a supportive and efficient educational environment.

Anti-Ragging Committee

Indira Gandhi Krishi Vishwavidyalaya (IGKV) takes a strong stance against ragging, ensuring a safe and supportive environment for all students. The university strictly adheres to the guidelines set by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and has a zero-tolerance policy towards any form of ragging. IGKV has established anti-ragging committees and squads to monitor and prevent such activities. Awareness programs and workshops are regularly conducted to educate students about the consequences of ragging. The university encourages students to report any incidents, assuring confidentiality and swift action against perpetrators to maintain a harmonious campus atmosphere.
