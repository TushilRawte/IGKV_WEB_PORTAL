Overview

Indira Gandhi Krishi Vishwavidyalaya (IGKV) admission process embodies a commitment to excellence and inclusivity. With a focus on agricultural education and research, IGKV welcomes aspiring students from diverse backgrounds to join its vibrant academic community. The admission procedure involves a comprehensive evaluation of applicants' academic achievements, entrance exam scores, and personal statements. Through transparent and merit-based selection criteria, IGKV ensures fairness and equal opportunities for all candidates. With state-of-the-art facilities, renowned faculty, and a rich academic tradition, IGKV offers a conducive environment for students to pursue their academic and research aspirations in the field of agriculture.
