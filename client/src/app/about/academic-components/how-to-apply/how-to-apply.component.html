Admission Portal

The IGKV Admission Portal is a comprehensive online platform designed to streamline the application and admission process for prospective students. It provides easy access to information on various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs offered by the university. Applicants can conveniently fill out and submit their forms, track application status, and receive important updates. The portal ensures a user-friendly experience, with clear instructions and support for each step. Additionally, it offers details on eligibility criteria, fee structures, and important dates, making the admission process transparent and accessible for all aspiring students.

UG Admission Procedures

Step-01  

Counseling Process
  •  In counseling process, firstly you need to fill application form with all documents needed. Register onto igkv.ac.in with basic details (Email ID, Name, Country, Mobile Number, Gender and Password).
  •   You should ensure to register using the correct email id and mobile no. as all further communication will be through email and mobile no. only.
  •   You should ensure that the application form is duly filled and the documentary evidence of the qualifying exam supporting the admission (if any) is also uploaded on igkv.ac.in
  •   For all the latest updates related to admission, visit igkv.ac.in from time to time.

Step-02  

Merit List
  •  Based on application form filled in counseling, Merit List will be uploaded.
  •  Please visit igkv.ac.in for other details.

Step-03  

Seat Allotment & DVC
  •   Based on Merit List , Seat will be allotted and DVC will be held.

Step-04  

Fee Payment & Seat Confirmation
  •   After DVC, Candidate need to pay the fee for the desired course.
  •   After successfull payment , seat will be confirmed and have to visit to the alloted college/department.

PG Admission Procedures

Step-01  

Fill CET (Common Entrance Test) Online Application Form & Upload Documents
  •   Register onto igkv.ac.in with basic details (Email ID, Name, Country, Mobile Number, Gender and Password).
  •   You should ensure to register using the correct email id and mobile no. as all further communication will be through email and mobile no. only.
  •   After registration, select the programme of your interest and fill the application form.
  •   You should ensure that the application form is duly filled and the documentary evidence of the qualifying exam supporting the admission (if any) is also uploaded on igkv.ac.in
  •   For all the latest updates related to admission, visit igkv.ac.in from time to time.

Step-02  

CET Exam Scheduling
  •  After successfully submitting application form,applied candidates will be alloted admit card ,roll no. & exam center within a few days
  •  Please visit igkv.ac.in  for the admit card and other details.

Step-03  

Result Declaration & Merit List
  •   After completion of the examination result will be declared.
  •   Based on the CET Examination Result, Merit List Will be declared.

Step-04  

Counseling Process
  •  In counseling process, firstly you need to fill application form with all documents needed.
  •   Based on application form filled in counseling, Merit List will be uploaded.
  •   Based on Merit List , Seat will be allotted and DVC will be held.
  •   After DVC, Candidate need to pay the fee for the desired course.
  •   After successfull payment , seat will be confirmed and have to visit to the alloted college/department.
