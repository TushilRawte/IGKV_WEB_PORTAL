Overview

Indira Gandhi Krishi Vishwavidyalaya( also known as IGKV) has an excellent library for the benefit of students, faculties and researchers. As one of the best established libraries according to International Standards, the Nehru Library has a large collection of Text and Reference books covering various branches of Agriculture,Horticulture and many more fields. The library is nearly three decades old. It is completely automated with RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) technology, Centralized Air Conditioned (AC), well protected with CCTV Security systems and fire alarms.
