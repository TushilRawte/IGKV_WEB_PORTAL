Overview

The College of Agriculture at the university is a dynamic academic institution dedicated to advancing agricultural sciences and fostering innovation in sustainable farming practices. Offering a diverse range of undergraduate and graduate programs, the college equips students with comprehensive knowledge in agronomy, animal science, horticulture, and agribusiness. Faculty members engage in cutting-edge research, addressing contemporary challenges in food security, environmental conservation, and rural development. The college emphasizes hands-on learning through fieldwork and encourages students to participate in internships and cooperative programs with industry partners. Graduates are well-prepared to contribute to the global agricultural landscape, promoting responsible and efficient practices for a sustainable future.
