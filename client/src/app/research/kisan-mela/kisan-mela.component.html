Overview

Indira Gandhi Krishi Vishwavidyalaya (IGKV) plays a pivotal role in promoting innovation and new technologies in agriculture through its annual Kisan Mela. This event serves as a vital platform for farmers to gain insights into the latest advancements in agricultural practices, tools, and technologies. IGKV's Kisan Mela showcases a wide array of innovations, from modern farming techniques to cutting-edge equipment designed to enhance productivity and sustainability. Through live demonstrations, expert-led workshops, and interactive sessions, farmers are introduced to the benefits of adopting new technologies, such as precision farming, organic practices, and water-saving irrigation methods. IGKV also emphasizes the importance of sustainable agriculture, encouraging farmers to adopt practices that protect the environment while boosting crop yields. The Kisan Mela fosters a collaborative environment where farmers can interact with scientists, agricultural experts, and fellow farmers, facilitating the exchange of knowledge and experiences. By highlighting success stories and providing hands-on experiences, IGKV motivates farmers to embrace innovation, ultimately leading to improved livelihoods and a more resilient agricultural sector. Through the Kisan Mela, IGKV reinforces its commitment to empowering the farming community with the knowledge and tools needed to thrive in a rapidly changing agricultural landscape.
