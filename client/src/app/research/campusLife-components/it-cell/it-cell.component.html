About IT Cell

The IT Cell of Indira Gandhi Krishi Vishwavidyalaya (IGKV) in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, serves as the technological backbone of the institution, facilitating seamless integration of digital solutions into academic and administrative processes. It oversees the implementation and maintenance of cutting-edge IT infrastructure, ensuring optimal functionality and efficiency across campus operations. From managing databases to providing technical support, the IT Cell plays a pivotal role in enhancing the university's digital ecosystem. Through its innovative initiatives and proactive approach, the cell empowers students, faculty, and staff with access to state-of-the-art technology, fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, and digital literacy within the IGKV community.
