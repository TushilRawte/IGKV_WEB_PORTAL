Committed to Safety & Security

At IGKV, we are deeply committed to fostering a culture of safety and security within our campus community. Our comprehensive security measures encompass state-of-the-art technology, highly trained personnel, and proactive strategies aimed at mitigating risks and ensuring the well-being of all individuals on our premises. From stringent access control protocols to regular security assessments and awareness campaigns, we prioritize the safety and security of our students, faculty, staff, and visitors. Through continuous improvement and collaboration with local authorities, we strive to maintain a safe and supportive environment where everyone can thrive and pursue their academic and personal goals with confidence.

CCTV & Electronic Surveillance

At IGKV, our commitment to security extends to the implementation of cutting-edge CCTV and electronic surveillance systems. Strategically positioned across our campus, these advanced technologies provide comprehensive monitoring of key areas in real-time, bolstering our ability to detect and deter security threats effectively. With continuous surveillance, we ensure the safety and well-being of our campus community, while also enhancing overall security measures. Our investment in CCTV and electronic surveillance underscores our dedication to proactive security practices, fostering a safe and secure environment where students, faculty, staff, and visitors can thrive with confidence and peace of mind.
