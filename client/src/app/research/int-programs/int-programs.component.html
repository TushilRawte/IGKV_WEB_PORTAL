Nano Programs

Nano Courses provide students an opportunity for getting an additional Certification through workshops, weekend classes etc. Nano courses are designed for busy professionals or for young managers who wish to get state-of-the-art knowledge of a new subject that is high in demand in the Industry. Courses usually run in weekends on an intensive basis.
