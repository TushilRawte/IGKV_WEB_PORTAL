Objectives

  • To provide testing and diagnostic services for soil, plant and livestock health etc.
  • To disseminate information and create awareness among the farmers about improved agricultural practices and diversification of farming.
  • Educate growers through published literature, audio-visual aid and by making visit to ongoing farm experiments of different departments.
  • To enable the organizations to generate resources through sale of their products, technologies and services for scaling up the activities without much dependence on plan funds.
  • To get feed-back from the user to frame research programme in future.

Introduction

  • To provide a ‘Single window’ delivery system for the products and species available from an institution to the farmers and other interested groups as a process of innovativeness in technology dissemination at the institute level.
  • To facilitate direct access to the farmers to the institutional resources available in terms of technology, advice, technology products etc., for reducing technology dissemination losses.
  • Educate growers through published literature, audio-visual aid and by making visit to ongoing farm experiments of different departments.
  • To provide mechanism for feed-beak from the user to the institute.
