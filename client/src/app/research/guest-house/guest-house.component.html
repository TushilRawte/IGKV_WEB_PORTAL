Dr. Prashant Kumar Pandey

In-charge

As the in-charge of the guest house, my primary responsibility is to ensure that all our guests have a comfortable and pleasant stay. We strive to maintain cleanliness, provide quality amenities, and ensure that all guest needs are met promptly. From managing reservations to coordinating with housekeeping and ensuring proper maintenance of the facilities, we focus on creating a welcoming atmosphere. Our team is dedicated to offering the best possible service, whether guests are here for a short visit or an extended stay. We value feedback and continuously work to enhance the guest experience.
