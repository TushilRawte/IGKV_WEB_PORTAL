Adhaar Verification Guidelines

I want to draw your attentaion towards the secure QR Code, which is incorporated in every Adhaar letter, PVC card or downloaded e-Adhaar. This QR code is temper-proof and digitally signed by UIDAI and in this form data capsule that contains the essential demographic details & photograph of the Adhaar Number holder. This QR code can scanned by using the official Adhaar QR Scannner app , Belove the steps how can use the scanner app

  • Download the official Adhaar QR Scanner app from google play store or direct use UIDAI official website (Desktop): click here
  • Open the app and grant necessary permissions to access the camera.
  • Point your device's camera at the QR code on the Adhaar letter, or e-Adhaar.
  • The app will automatically scan the QR code and display the demographic details and photograph associated with the Adhaar number.
  • Verify that the information displayed matches the details of the Adhaar holder.
  • Works offline does not need internet connection to work

What happends if Adhaar documents is fake or altered

  • Ther scanner not will either not display any data, or
  • The data displayed from qr code won't match with the information printed on it
