Shri Ramen Deka Chancellor
  • The 10th Governor of Chhattisgarh, Shri Ramen Deka was born on 1 March 1954 in Sualkuchi, Kamrup (R), Assam. His father's name is Late Shri Surendra Nath Deka and mother's name is Late Smt. Champabati Deka. He received his B.A. degree in Philosophy and Economics from Pragjyotish College, Guwahati University, Assam. His wife's name is Mrs. Ranee Deka kakoti.

  • Shri Ramen Deka is a senior politician. He has been active in politics since 1977. He was a founding member of the Bharatiya Janata Party in the year 1980. Shri Ramen Deka has also been the state president of Assam BJP in the year 2006. While working in BJP, he has also handled many responsibilities at the national level. He was Vice Chairman of State Innovation and Transformation Aayog , Assam (Cabinet Rank) from August 2021 to 29 July 2024.
